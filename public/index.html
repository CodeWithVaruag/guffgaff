Community Guidelines

To maintain a safe and enjoyable environment for all users, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Respect Others: Treat all users with kindness. Personal attacks, harassment, and hate speech will not be tolerated.
  • No Spamming: Avoid sending repetitive messages or advertisements. Keep the chat relevant and engaging.
  • Privacy Matters: Do not share personal information (yours or others') in the chat. Protect your privacy and that of others.
  • Content Restrictions: Explicit content, including pornography and graphic violence, is strictly prohibited.
  • Stay On Topic: Keep discussions relevant to the chat's theme. Off-topic conversations should be taken to private messages.
  • Report Issues: If you encounter harassment or inappropriate behavior, please report it to the moderators immediately.
  • Have Fun: Enjoy the experience! Engage positively and make new friends.
